MOMO - More Music More Movies was developed with Slogan "A Daily Dose of Entertainment. MOMO aims to entertain users with a wide variety of entertainment content (Songs, Movies, Music Videos, Karaoke, FM, etc.) and very useful features like Voting, Event and Movie ticketing, RBT as well. The Application MOMO - More Music More Movies have more than 30,000+ Songs, 500 plus Movies, 1,000 plus trending Music Videos, Karaoke features (users can give a sound in the Song’s track as well as in Music Videos with subtitle). MOMO aims to cater to the wide range of people from all age groups and from various regions by providing them with supreme features. Voting Campaign with SaReGaMaPa Li’L Champs Nepal [Season 1] and Nepal Idol [Season 4] was a huge success too. This application is the result of collaboration between New IT Venture Corporation - Japan and Music Nepal. MOMO - More Music More Movies App is designed to facilitate the following purposes:- • Songs, Movies, Music Videos, Karaoke • FM and more.
